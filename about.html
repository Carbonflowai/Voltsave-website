About Voltsave AI

Energy cost control, built for energy-intensive businesses.

Voltsave AI is an AI-enabled business energy optimisation platform, designed to accelerate the demand-side energy transition.

We exist to help businesses transform energy from an unpredictable expense into a structured, manageable, and performance-driven lever.

Why We Built Voltsave

Energy has become one of the largest and least predictable operating costs for businesses.

At the same time:

  • Over 40% of emissions reductions required by 2030 must come from energy efficiency improvements.
  • SMEs account for a significant share of commercial energy demand.
  • Yet most lack structured tools to manage energy with financial discipline.

Traditional solutions are either enterprise-focused, procurement-driven, or disconnected from profitability.

Voltsave was built to close that gap.

The Economic Case for Energy Efficiency

Energy efficiency is not just a sustainability objective, it is a financial imperative.

Unlocking efficiency across UK SMEs could contribute over 22Mt of potential CO₂ savings while strengthening margins and improving operational resilience.

Voltsave helps businesses translate that systemic opportunity into measurable cost control.

Our Mission

To give businesses the tools to manage energy as strategically without complex infrastructure or heavy upfront investment.

Our Vision

A future where energy costs are predictable, controllable, and aligned with business performance.
